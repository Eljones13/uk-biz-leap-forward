Registering your company doesn't need to be complicated. With **BusinessBuilder Pro**, you can go from idea to incorporation in just a few clicks.

## Step 1: Start the Wizard
- Go to the [Company Formation Page](/wizard).  
- Click **Start My Company**.  
- Select company type: Ltd, CIC, or Partnership.

## Step 2: Enter Details
- Add your company name (check availability).  
- Provide registered office.  
- Add directors and shareholders.

## Step 3: Generate Documents
- Auto-fills IN01, Articles, PSC Register.  
- Preview before filing.

## Step 4: File With Companies House
- In demo: mock confirmation.  
- In live: direct filing.  
- Get Certificate of Incorporation.
